A Nameless Dark

A Nameless Dark is the first book in what will become the Nameless Dark series. It’s set in modern-day, midwestern America, and centers around the Acheson family.

Jonas Acheson believes he knows what it means to be a Holder of Light, but when the power protecting his family is broken, Jonas learns that everything he’s been told is a lie and is forced to run for his life, hunted by enemies ancient and terrifying. Driven from their home in rural Ohio, seventeen-year-old Jonas and his siblings, Samuel and Lara, are pursued by the evil Morrighan and her relentless tracker, Wiley, who serve the Nameless Dark. With help from old allies, they set out to find the one person who may be able to contact the other Families of Light: a mysterious, ageless scotsman named Graver. They’re accompanied by two fellow Holders of Light: Thaddeus, an experienced soldier from an African Family of Light, and Jian Shu Lin, a young Chinese girl who is a fierce warrior and Graver’s adopted daughter (the source her Scottish accent).

From a frozen tower in downtown Pittsburgh, to a genuine medieval castle in the Colorado Rocky Mountains, the Achesons seek the elusive Graver, and Jonas searches for the truth behind his family’s legacy. But Wiley’s monsters seem to find them at every turn, dragging the Acheson kids deeper into a battle their family abandoned long ago. When their Uncle Aaron is captured while covering an escape, the stakes are ratcheted even higher. To save Aaron and stop the Morrighan from destroying his family, Jonas will have to master his use of Light, overcome his guilt from past failures, and find the confidence to stand beside his siblings and fight against all odds.
